Comprehensive Engineering Solutions

Engineering Simulation & Optimization

  • Simulate granular material behaviour for industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, mining, and bulk material handling.
  • Integrate with Finite Element Analysis (FEA) and Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) for advanced performance assessment.

Finite Element Analysis (FEA) & Multi-Physics Simulations

Improve Product Reliability with FEA

  • Validate complex mechanical systems, predict failure modes, and enhance reliability. Support VAVE initiatives.

Mechanical Product Design:

Innovate and accelerate product development.

End-to-end mechanical design solutions, from concept to detailed engineering. Specializes in Design for Manufacturability (DFM). Uses advanced CAD tools.

Electrical Design & Engineering

  • Develops electrical layouts and schematics for low- and medium-voltage applications.
  • Designs and optimizes panels, MCCs, PCCs, transformers, relays, and power distribution systems.
  • Ensures compliance with international standards (IEC, NEMA, CSA, UL).
  • Supports industrial automation, protection systems, and energy management.

Manufacturing & Panel Engineering

  • Designs industrial control panels per client specifications.
  • Provides detailed engineering for PLC, VSD/VFD, MCC, PCC, and junction boxes.
  • Vendor coordination, cataloguing, and compliance to global standards.

Reverse Engineering: Modernize legacy designs.

  • Converts 3D scan data into CAD models for legacy parts and prototype development.
  • Conducts wear analysis and inspection against original specifications.
  • Facilitates tooling verification and additive manufacturing integration.

Drives Engineering: Maximize performance

  • Designs and integrates LV & MV AC/DC drive systems.
  • Standalone and sectional drive applications across multiple industries.
  • Provides system architecture, sizing, protection, and performance optimization.

Automation Engineering: Enchance efficiency with automation

  • Develops industrial automation solutions for metals, material handling, mining, renewables, and more.
  • Specializes in PLC programming, networking, control philosophy development, and system integration.
  • Implements SCADA and HMI configurations for seamless operator interaction.

User Experience (HMI & SCADA Systems): Optimize Your Operator Interface

  • Enhances user experience through intuitive HMI and SCADA solutions.
  • Develops custom mimics, dashboards, and plant layouts.
  • Implements ergonomic and user-friendly designs for operational efficiency.

Energy Management: Reduce costs, improve sustainability.

  • Provides energy monitoring, control, and conservation solutions for industrial and commercial clients.
  • Conducts site surveys to assess energy consumption patterns and optimize efficiency.
  • Implements power-saving strategies to reduce operational costs and improve sustainability.

Supply Chain Services: Optimize global sourcing.

  • Assists in vendor assessment, selection, and onboarding for global sourcing strategies.
  • Supports procurement management, contract compliance, and logistics optimization.
  • Provides quality assurance, inspection, and supplier risk analysis.
